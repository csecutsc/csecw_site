ABOUT US

Founded in 2017, CSEC-W is the Computer Science Enrichment Club’s Web Development Division. We are a group of passionate second-year Computer Science students seeking to teach transferable web development skills, such as HTML5, CSS3, and JavaScript, which can be used in conjunction to create a participant’s very first portfolio website. We operate on a weekly basis, with lectures occurring every Tuesday, from 6 pm to 8 pm. You can contact us through a variety of methods.
